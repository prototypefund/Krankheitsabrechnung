Infantile Seborrhoeic Dermatitis (Cradle Cap)
Wikipedia, GFDL, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image:Baby_With_Cradle_Cap.jpg

This is a brown scale, non-itchy with no inflammation. It occurs in
babies around the age of 3 months.

It is benign and self-limiting. The treatment is to moisten the scale with 
live oil and wipe it away.
