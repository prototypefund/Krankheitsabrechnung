Keloid (Proud Flesh)
Wikipedia, GFDL, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image:Scars-cropped.jpg

This is a hypertropic scar formed in response to trauma. hey are
more common in dark-skinned peoples.

They are non-malignant, but can be a problem cosmetically, treatment 
is difficult. Steroid injection, laser and cryotherapy have been described.
