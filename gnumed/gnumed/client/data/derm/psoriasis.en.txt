Psoriasis
Wikipedia, GFDL, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image:Psoriasis.jpg

Psoriasis is flat confluent itchy erythematous rash with fine silvery scale.
It is quite common, around 3% of the US population.

Initally treatment is with topical steroids. Other options are regular 
UVB light exposure, dithranol, and immunosuppressant agents in severe cases.

Guttate, erythodermic and pustular variants exist.
